Healthy Eating, Healthy Mind

Stay Well to Stay Strong

Good nutrition is a critical part of health and development. According to the World Health Organization (WHO), better nutrition is related to improved health especially during at time of COVID-19.The secret to good health lies in your plate. A healthy, balanced meal provides all the essential nutrients your body needs to stay healthy!

Include Healthy Fats

People should limit their intake of saturated fats while avoiding trans fats, according to the WHO. A person can replace these fats with unsaturated fats, which they can find in foods such as avocado, oily fish, and vegetable oils. The secret to good health lies in your plate. A healthy, balanced meal provides all the essential nutrients your body needs to stay healthy.

Eat Whole Grains

The American Heart Association (AHA) recommend people eat whole grains rather than refined grains.Whole grains contain nutrients such as B vitamins, iron, and fiber. These nutrients are essential for body functions that include carrying oxygen in the blood, regulating the immune system, and balancing blood sugar.

Reduce Sugar

According to research, dietary sugar, dextrose, and high fructose corn syrup may increase the risk of cardiovascular disease and metabolic syndrome.People should look out for hidden sugars in foods that manufacturers label as names ending in “-ose,” for example, fructose, sucrose, and glucose.Natural sugars, such as honey and maple syrup, could also contribute to weight gain if someone eats them too often.

Drink More Water

Water contains zero calories.It helps to increase the feeling of fullness and metabolism, thus aids in achieving healthy weight. Water carries oxygen and nutrients to the cells, and helps maintain immune funtion and combant any viruses.
